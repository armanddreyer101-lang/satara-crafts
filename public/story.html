Est. in the Lowveld · South Africa

Made by Hand,Made with Love

Satara Crafts was born from a simple passion — to make beautiful, useful things that bring joy into everyday life. Every piece carries a piece of that story.

Where it all began

Rooted in Passion

It started with a brush, some paint, and a kitchen table in the Lowveld. What began as a personal love for decorating everyday items slowly grew into something much bigger — a collection of handpainted pieces that people cherish as gifts, heirlooms, and daily treasures.

Every single item in our range is painted by hand. No two pieces are exactly alike, and that's exactly the point. When you buy from Satara Crafts, you're not buying something off a shelf — you're buying something that someone poured their heart into.

What we stand for

Our Values

100% Handmade

Every piece is individually painted and finished by hand. We never mass-produce — each item gets the time and attention it deserves.

Local Materials

We source glass, wood, and textiles from trusted South African suppliers. Sustainability guides every decision we make.

Perfect for Gifting

From weddings to birthdays to corporate gifts — we create pieces that make people feel truly seen and celebrated.

African Inspiration

Our designs draw from the colours, patterns, and spirit of Africa — bold, warm, and full of life.

How it's made

Hand Painting

Finishing

Packaged with Love

Meet the Artist

The Heart Behind Satara Crafts

Behind every brushstroke is a mother, artist, and dreamer who believes that beautiful things should be part of everyday life — not just saved for special occasions.

"I paint because it makes me happy. And if it makes someone else happy too, then that's everything."

Based in South Africa, she creates in small batches from her studio, taking on custom commissions and pouring genuine love into every piece she makes.
